The Eagle has landed

Neocities is a weird place, you know. There are so many different websites with so many different styles, aspirations, and goals. Some of them are very flashy and colorful and full of experimental strange features, but that is not my jam.

The goal of this website is not to blow your mind but to show information I deem interesting or useful (Either for myself or anyone who might be reading) in a concise and straightforwards clear manner. No 90s nostalgia, colorful rainbows, or strange javascript contraptions.

Nevertheless, this site still won't be following the average 2020 website conventions that plague the modern web either. I might write a blog post about my opinions on the design of websites in [current year] down the line, who knows.
